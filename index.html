Product Description

For CCTV Camera 48 LED IR Infrared 75FT Illuminator Night Vision DC Light Lamp

For use with a night vision surveillance camera.

A better effect can be obtained with illumination intensity less than 0.001 Lux Auto Power-On Sensor, which only turns on when the surrounding environment is dark.

When ambient light is either absent or too dim, infrared light illumination (or IR illumination) is used. When an IR camera's IR array is too narrowly focused or not powerful enough, an external IR light illumination can provide an additional source of invisible infrared light to improve night vision range.

When used with night vision equipment, it illuminates like a flashlight while remaining undetectable to the naked eye

Features:

  • Viewing distance: 20m
  • LED life time: over 100,000 hours
